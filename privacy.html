Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

The Edge ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use The Edge mental conditioning app ("App").

Information We Collect

When you use The Edge, we may collect the following information:

  • Name: Used to personalize your daily training sessions.
  • Sport selection: Used to deliver sport-specific mental conditioning content.
  • Email address: Used to manage your account and subscription.
  • Usage data: Session progress, streak data, and XP points to track your training journey.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Personalize your daily mental conditioning sessions
  • Track your training progress and streak
  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Improve the App's AI and content
  • Communicate with you about your account (if needed)

Subscriptions and Payments

The Edge offers a subscription service at $19.99/month after a free trial. All payment processing is handled securely by Apple (for iOS) or Google (for Android). We do not store your payment information.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share anonymized, aggregate data for analytical purposes. Your personal information is never shared with advertisers.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and we implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of any marketing communications

Children's Privacy

The Edge is designed for athletes and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
